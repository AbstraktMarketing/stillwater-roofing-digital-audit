The Local SEO Problem, in One Paragraph

Stillwater Roofing has a verified Google Business Profile at 4.9 stars across 34 reviews — that’s genuine credibility most smaller competitors don’t have. The GBP is the asset; the surrounding listing ecosystem is the problem. The old address — 2631 Interlachen Dr — is still propagating through at least five directories, and NAP inconsistency across the web directly undermines Google’s ability to trust and surface the business in the local pack. More critically, Bing Places has no listing at all, meaning Bing AI, Microsoft Copilot, and Bing Maps return nothing when a property manager searches for a commercial roofer on a Windows machine. With a strong GBP as the foundation, cleaning up the directory ecosystem and expanding to missing platforms creates a clear multiplier effect on local visibility.
